How this works

  • Schedule: a macOS launchd job runs generate-digest.sh daily at ~07:00 (see README.md).
  • Engine: prompt.md (what gets researched + the format). Sources: sources.md.
  • Hybrid backstop: also subscribe to Smol AI — AINews — the gold-standard automated daily — as a human-curated safety net.
  • Run on demand: cd ~/ai-digest && ./generate-digest.sh
  • Tune it: edit sources.md (what it reads) or prompt.md (depth/weighting/format).
